Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy applies to all use of Linggan Space AI, including account registration, room image uploads, AI image generation, credits, subscriptions, and support interactions.

Allowed Use

Linggan Space AI may be used only for lawful interior design, home renovation, furnishing visualization, and related room redesign scenarios.

Prohibited Content

  • No adult, pornographic, sexually explicit, nude, fetish, or otherwise NSFW content.
  • No content involving minors in sexualized, exploitative, or inappropriate contexts.
  • No hateful, violent, extremist, abusive, or harassing content.
  • No illegal activity, fraud, impersonation, deception, or infringement of third-party rights.
  • No attempts to use the service to generate unsafe, deceptive, or policy-evading content.

Uploads and Prompts

  • You may upload only images you own or have permission to use.
  • You may not submit prompts or reference materials intended to create prohibited content.
  • We may screen prompts and related generation requests with automated safety systems before model execution.

Enforcement

We may refuse requests, suspend generation, remove content, freeze credits, cancel subscriptions, or terminate accounts that violate this policy or applicable law.
