Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Linggan Space AI provides an online AI interior design service that lets users upload room photos and generate design concepts. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how customers can contact us.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your email address and encrypted password when you register.
  • Room images and related generation data that you upload to use the service.
  • Order, billing, and subscription records related to plan purchases.
  • Basic technical data such as IP address, browser information, and service logs used for security and troubleshooting.
  • Moderation and safety review data associated with generation requests.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process AI image generations and return results to you.
  • To manage payments, credits, subscriptions, and order history.
  • To improve service quality, detect abuse, and maintain platform security.
  • To respond to support questions and customer service requests.
  • To perform content safety checks and prevent prohibited or abusive use of the platform.

Payments

Payments may be processed by third-party payment providers. We do not store full payment card details on our website. Payment providers may collect and process billing information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the website, process payments, host infrastructure, moderate prompts, run AI generation, or deliver customer support, only as needed to provide the service.

Data Retention

We retain account, order, and service data for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Choices

You may contact us to ask questions about your personal information or request account-related assistance. If you would like help with deletion or corrections, please email our support team.
